About Us

Mission and Vision

LLM360 enables community-owned AGI by creating standards and tools to advance the bleeding edge of LLM capability and empower knowledge transfer, research, and development. We believe in a future where artificial general intelligence (AGI) is created by the community, for the community. Through an open ecosystem of equitable computational resources, high quality data, and flowing technical knowledge, we can ensure ethical AGI development and universal access for all innovators.
